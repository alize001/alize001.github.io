Final Artwork
For this project we got to take everything we learned and create something of our choice, but keeping in mind Mark Tribe's definition of new media art.For this project I wanted to focus on the depression/mental health that a-lot of people are going through during this time of quarantine/COVID. At first I was going to only focus on younger adults but after researching some more info,I found out its every one of every age, mostly hitting our medical workers. So, I wanted to show the struggle that nurses/doctors are going through and as well as families. To get the feel of anxiety or feeling overwhelming I took some photos into Audacity and manipulated them. Then I added some more filters and warped the photos in photoshop.
